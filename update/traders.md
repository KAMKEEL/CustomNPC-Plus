# Trader Enhancements

Traders upgraded with a **stock system** and **currency integration** for proper shops with limited supply and real economy support.

## Stock System
**Stock Modes:** Global (shared pool) or Per-Player (individual purchase limits).
**Restock Timers:** MC Daily/Weekly, RL Daily/Weekly, or Custom (MC ticks / real-time).
> Stock counts, out-of-stock indicators, and restock timers are visible in the trade interface. Admins can manually reset stock/cooldowns via the Trader GUI.

## Currency Trades
Trades can require **currency on top of items** — e.g., a diamond sword costing 3 diamonds **plus** 500 coins.
