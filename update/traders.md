# Trader Enhancements

---

Traders have been upgraded with a **stock system** and **currency integration**, turning them into proper shops with limited supply and real economy support.

---

## Stock System

Traders now support **limited stock** with automatic restocking.

**Stock Modes:**
- **Global** - All players share the same stock pool. When an item sells out, it's gone for everyone until restock
- **Per-Player** - Each player has their own purchase limits. One player buying out stock doesn't affect others

**Restock Timers:**
- **MC Daily / Weekly** - Restock based on Minecraft time
- **RL Daily / Weekly** - Restock based on real-life time
- **Custom** - Set your own interval in MC ticks or real-time

Stock counts, out-of-stock indicators, and restock timers are all visible directly in the trade interface so players know exactly what's available.

> Admins can manually reset stock or cooldowns via the Trader GUI.

---

## Currency Trades

Trades can now require **currency on top of items**. Combine material costs with coin costs for balanced economy progression.

For example, a diamond sword might cost 3 diamonds **plus** 500 coins — creating more meaningful economic choices.

---

## Balance Preview

A **client-side balance preview** shows your expected balance during trades, so you can see exactly how much you'll have left before confirming a purchase.
